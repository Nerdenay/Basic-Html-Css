Transmutation

The transmutation Nen type seems to borrow the best qualities from both the conjuration and emission types. Two of the strongest characters in Hunter X Hunter are practitioners of this type of Nen which speaks to its strength more than any description could.Both Killua and Hisoka demonstrate incredible transmutation abilities by using their aura to create powers and abilities unlike anything that exists in the physical world. Killua mimics the properties of lightning in order to enhance the strength of his attacks, while Hisoka invents an entirely new substance by combining the properties of both rubber and gum. Much like the enhancement type, this ability had both offensive and defensive capabilities, but because it requires a sharp mind to truly take advantage of, it doesn't quite take the crown of the most powerful Nen type.
